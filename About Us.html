Our Story

Founded in 2014, Pothinus Carl Projects is a proudly 100% black-owned transportation company committed to providing reliable, efficient, affordable and innovative transport solutions throughout South Africa. Since our inception, we have experienced rapid growth, particularly within the telecommunications, mining, and oil & gas industries, among other industries we have partnered with.


What distinguishes Pothinus Carl Projects is our dedication to building long-term partnerships founded on reliable, responsive, affordable, safe, and world-class service, along with our capability to create customized service offerings tailored to each client's unique needs. Regardless of the logistical requirements, the size of the business, or location, we are proud to provide our clients with robust, reliable, and customized solutions.
